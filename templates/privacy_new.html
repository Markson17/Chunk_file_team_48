Thanks for entrusting GO-chunk with your files and your personal information. Handling your data is a serious responsibility, and we want you to know how we go about doing that.

Introduction

We are GO-chunk, a provider of file splitting services based in Nigeria. Our company number is 7657546. This Privacy Policy lets you know what happens to any personal data that you give to us or that we collect from you. We collect only the minimum amount of personal information necessary for you to make use of our Services; we ensure that we meet our responsibilities of the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA), along with the Data Protection Act 2020 (DPA 2020); and we will only ever use your data as this Privacy Policy describes. We are registered with the Nigeria Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) in the Register of Data Controllers (registration number Z1127652).

What type of data do we collect?

We do not intentionally collect or store special categories of personal data, such as genetic data, health information, or religious information. Although GO-chunk does not request or intentionally collect any special categories of personal data, we realize that you might store this kind of information in your account, via the files that you convert.

If you ask us to convert or store any files containing special categories of personal data on our servers, located in the Nigeria, we will only do so on your instructions as a data processor and in accordance with our Data Processing Agreement with you. You are the controller of this data and you must ensure that you have a legal basis to share this data with us.

How do we manage your data?

GO-chunk takes the security of data very seriously, and we work hard to protect any information you provide to us from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access or disclosure. We take all reasonable precautions to safeguard the confidentiality of your personal information, including through use of appropriate organisational and technical measures. These measures take into account the sensitivity of the data we collect, process and store, and the current state of technology.

Given the nature of communications and information processing technology, GO-chunck guarantees that information, during transmission through the Internet or while stored on our systems or otherwise in our care, will be absolutely safe from intrusion by others, we will do our utmost to protect it. Once we have received your personal information, we will use strict procedures and security features to prevent unauthorised access to it.

As a minimum we take the following measures to secure your data:
Physical Security - GO-chunk infrastructure is only hosted in data centres which meet rigorous security standards. Physical access is strictly controlled both at the perimeter and at building ingress points by professional security staff utilising video surveillance, intrusion detection systems, and other electronic means.

Data Transfer Integrity - All traffic to and from GO-chunk servers is secured by transport level security (TLS) sent over a Secure Socket Layer (SSL), and secured using an AES 256-bit SSL certificate. This ensures that data sent between your systems and ours is encrypted using military grade encryption.

Password Encryption - GO- chunk user account passwords are stored in our database after being salted and hashed using the Bcrypt encryption algorithm.

Firewalls - GO-chunk enforces network level control for access to infrastructure by using multiple different firewall technologies to ensure that different components of its systems are logically isolated from one another.

Operational Access Controls - GO-chunk employees require access to production services for operational reasons. We employ multiple authentication mechanisms to ensure that production systems are accessed only by authorised members of staff and are protected from unauthorised access.

Software Updates - GO-chunk regularly applies software patches to production infrastructure in order to ensure a strong security posture to known software vulnerabilities.

External Accreditation - GO-chunk takes a pro-active approach to security by employing an external company to perform monthly security scans of its infrastructure.

Bug Bounty Program - GO-chunk operates an informal "bug bounty" program that encourages security researchers to perform limited and authorised testing of the integrity of GO-chunk systems.

How do we share your data?

We may share personal information with a limited number of third-party vendors who process it on our behalf in order to provide or improve our Services, and who have agreed to privacy restrictions and security obligations consistent with this Privacy Policy.

We may share personal information where it is in our legitimate interests to do so to run, grow and develop our business if we are involved in a merger, sale, or acquisition, including a bankruptcy sale. If any such change of ownership happens, we will ensure it is under terms that preserve the confidentiality of personal information, and we will notify you on our website or by email before any transfer of your personal information.
